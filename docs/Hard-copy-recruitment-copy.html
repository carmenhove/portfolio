Help us better understand maternal postpartum health
Whos eligible? We are recruiting individuals between
the ages of 18 and 45 who are in the 3rd trimester of
pregnancy or within the first 6 months of the postpartum
period. If youre currently pregnant, we will schedule your
data collection for after you give birth.
What does participation involve? Over a single
24-hour period of your choosing, youll be asked to provide
a morning and evening saliva sample, keep a simple record
of how and when you feed your infant, and complete an
online questionnaire. Well also ask you to provide two
finger-prick blood spot samples, but this part is optional.
Where? In your own home. Materials will be mailed to
you and samples will be collected by the researcher via
curbside pickup.
Will I receive compensation? Yes, youll be offered a $40 Visa Cash
Card or Amazon gift card (or $50, if you opt-in to the blood collection portion).
Youll also be given access to your research data (salivary hormone levels and
immune profile).
