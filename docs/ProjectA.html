Project A

What are the sex-specific effects of age on hormone production?

Estrogen and testosterone are often described as female and male hormones, respectively, but both sexes produce each hormone (just at different concentrations).

In this for-fun project, I utilize publicly available data collected by the National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey (NHANES) to model the sex-specific effects of age on estradiol and testosterone.

Testosterone (LBXTST) and Estradiol (LBXEST) measured in both males and females ages 6 and up during 2014-2015 and 2015-2016 collection cycles. Testosterone as ng/dL, estradiol was pg/mL. Detection limit data also collected as a comment code, indicating whether or not detection limit threshold was met.
